Explore Our Government Schemes

  • Pradhan Mantri Ayushman Bharat Health Infrastructure Mission (PM-ABHIM): Aims to improve healthcare infrastructure, especially in rural areas, ensuring accessible and affordable healthcare for all citizens.
  • Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana (PM-Kisan): Provides financial support to farmers, helping them meet agricultural expenses through direct cash transfers.
  • PM Vishwakarma Yojana: Focuses on skill development for artisans, offering financial support for preserving traditional crafts.
  • Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY): Offers affordable housing to economically weaker sections, aiming for "Housing for All" by providing financial aid.
  • Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana: Provides free LPG connections to families below the poverty line to promote clean cooking.
  • Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY): Encourages states to increase agricultural productivity and improve rural development.
